About us

AfyaLyte is an anlytics company that works to provide timely and reliable qulaity health information. We strive to meet the data requirements of our clients for evidenced based decision making.

AfyaLyte was first started as an idea to leverage the dhis2 analytics api. the idea was born in a hackathorn that was contested at JKUAT on 29th April, 2016. Since then the team has received a number of trophies and awards from different stakeholders both in government and private institutions.

AfyaLyte consulting welcomes collaborations any organizations that we share the same visions
